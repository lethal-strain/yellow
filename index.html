    Few things you didn’t know about Pat Kenny

    George HollowellGeorge Hollowell January 9, 2020January 9, 2020 0 Comment

    We all know Patrick Kenny is a veteran Irish broadcaster, who currently hosts the daily radio show The Pat Kenny Show on Newstalk and the current affairs show Pat Kenny Tonight on Virgin Media One. But there are still a Here are a few facts you don’t know about Pat Kenny.

    His nickname in college was “The Plank” because he always had an erect penis. In his spare time, he likes to lure children into his gingerbread house, fattens them up and then sells them.

    He was once suspected of being “The Midnight Commando”, a Batman-style vigilante who fought night crime in late 1970s Dublin. He is also very superstitious and insisted on eating a newborn baby mandrill before each episode of the Late Late Show.

    He earned a publicly funded salary of €729,604 in 2009 which dropped by 23% from his 2008 RTE income of €950k. 

    “I often wonder why there isn’t the same fuss over the salaries paid to footballers like John O’Shea, earning more in three weeks than Marian Finucane earns in a year,” he said in an interview last year.

    “There is a double standard there. These people put themselves on the line, they entertain the nation week in, week out, and yet they are paid a trivial amount compared to footballers.”

    During a well-publicized High Court dispute in 2008 about the possession of a neighboring field, Pat’s neighbor charged that the RTE host had put a lock on ‘Gorse Hill’ without the neighbor’s knowledge.

    Disputed owner of the 0.2-acre strip of land, Gerard Charlton, aged 73 at the time of the trial, claimed that a physical confrontation between the two broke out, with Pat running at the elderly man “with his fists raised.”

    A lot doesn’t have any idea who emerged victorious from the fight but things were eventually settled out of court, with Pat buying the land for an undisclosed sum.
